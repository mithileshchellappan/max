## Publishing Max

Max is a fork of Bruno and must be published under its own name, icon, bundle identifiers, package names, and distribution channels.

Before publishing:

- Keep `license.md` and the upstream copyright notice.
- Keep `NOTICE.md` with fork attribution.
- Do not publish `.env.local` files or Convex deployment secrets.
- Replace placeholder repository, support, and security-contact links with the real public repo details.
- Use the Max app icon and avoid Bruno trademarks or official Bruno distribution channels.
- For macOS, sign with Developer ID Application, notarize the DMG, and staple the ticket.

Do not publish this fork as Bruno or imply that it is an official Bruno release.
